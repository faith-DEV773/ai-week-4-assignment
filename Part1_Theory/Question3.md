Q3: Why Bias Mitigation is Critical in AI-Powered UX Personalization

Bias mitigation in user experience (UX) personalization is critical because biased AI systems can:

Discriminate against marginalized groups (race, gender, age, disability)
Perpetuate stereotypes and limit user opportunities
Violate regulations (GDPR, ADA, Civil Rights Act)
Damage brand reputation and user trust
Create echo chambers that reinforce harmful patterns
